WHAT IS A MINDSET ?

Mindsets are beliefs about yourself and your basic qualities. Think about your talents, beliefs and intelligence. Do you think these qualities are fixed or do you think that you could change these things if you wanted to?

KNOWING AND CHOOSING A MINDSET

KINDS OF MINDSET

1. FIXED MINDSET

A fixed mindset reflects a belief in natural talent: that qualities such as intelligence, creativity, and talent are predetermined and finite. These qualities are so set that whatever you lack, you will continue to lack.

2. GROWTH MINDSET

A growth mindset is a belief that basic abilities can continue to be developed through hardwork and dedication. These innate qualities are just starting points with success being the fruit of effort, learning, and persistence. It involves the ability to accept change and grow with it.

SCENARIO 1

You send someone a text and it has been an hour and they still haven't responded. How do you respond ?

Fixed Mindset: I guess i'm not just important to them. That's why they are not answering

Growth Mindset: Maybe their phone died. Maybe they are in the middle of a conversation and it has nothing to do with me

SENERIO 2

You spent all night working on a project inorder to meet up a deadline. The next day a team member who has made little to no contribution on the project gives you a negative feedback. How do you respond ?

Fixed Mindset: They never appreciate the hard work i put in. This is going to be last time i put in this much effort to a project

Growth Mindset: Seek clarity on this feedback, view the feedback as an oppurtunity for growth

SCENARIO 3

You are the lead in a new project the company took on. You have a workflow planned. You have decided on a design pattern. You have also picked out a technology you feel is best for the project. Now you and your team are having a meeting on how to commence the project. To your surprise, your team does not support your seemingly well thought out idea giving you reasons why they feel things should be done differently. How do do you respond ?

WHAT IS YOUR MINDSET

A Person's mindset canbe determined by his/her reaction to:

  • Criticism

  • Challenges / Obstacles

  • Effort

  • Success of others

BENEFIT OF A GROWTH MINDSET

  • Enjoy life, even when you are not good at it

  • Improve your self-insight and self-esteem

  • Improve your relationships

  • Be better at taking responsibility for your life

  • Strengthen your confidence
